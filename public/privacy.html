Privacy Policy for ordcafe.io

Effective date: 21 March 2024

Introduction

At ordcafe.io, accessible from ordcafe.io, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by ordcafe.io and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

Information We Collect

The personal information that you are asked to provide, and the reasons why you are asked to provide it, will be made clear to you at the point we ask you to provide your personal information.

We do not collect any personal information from our users. Our platform only collects data in the form of words input into the cloud for the creation of word clouds and the number of users present in a language cafe session.

Use of Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the functionality of the ordcafe.io platform to:

  • Create word clouds based on user inputs during language cafe sessions.
  • Monitor the number of users in a room to ensure proper service functionality.
